Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What documents do I need to prepare before applying? ▼
You'll typically need educational certificates, identity proof (Aadhar, PAN), caste/community certificates (if applicable), domicile certificate (for Pondicherry residents), recent passport-sized photographs, and your scanned signature. Always refer to the official notification for the exact list.
Can I edit my application after submission? ▼
Generally, once an application is submitted, major edits are not allowed. Some portals might offer a limited window for minor corrections. It's crucial to review your application thoroughly before final submission.
What if I forget my Login ID or Password? ▼
The official recruitment portal usually has a "Forgot Login ID" or "Forgot Password" option. You can typically retrieve them by providing your registered mobile number or email ID.
Is there a fee for applying to these government jobs? ▼
Most government job applications have a fee, though it varies by post and category. Some categories (e.g., SC/ST, PwD, Women) might be exempt. Always check the official notification for fee details.
Where can I find the latest job notifications? ▼
You can find the latest job notifications on our Current Openings page, as well as directly on the Official Puducherry Recruitment Portal.
