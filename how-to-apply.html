Step-by-Step Application Process

1. Visit the Portal

Go to https://recruitment.py.gov.in

2. Select Recruitment

Click on the name of the recruitment you wish to apply for.

3. Register

  • Click Register.
  • Enter your mobile number and the security code displayed.
  • Enter the OTP received on your phone.

4. Receive Login Credentials

You will get your Login ID and Password via SMS.
