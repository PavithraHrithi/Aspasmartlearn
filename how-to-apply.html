How to Apply for Pondicherry Government Jobs

Follow these easy steps to complete your online application at the official recruitment portal of the Government of Puducherry.

  1. Visit: https://recruitment.py.gov.in
  2. Select Recruitment: Click on the job post you wish to apply for.
  3. Register:
    • Enter your mobile number and captcha.
    • Verify using OTP.
  4. Login Credentials: You’ll receive your Login ID and Password via SMS.
  5. Change Password: Log in and update your password.
  6. Apply/Edit: Click “Apply/Edit” against your selected post and fill out the form in sections:

Application Form Sections

  • 1. Basic Details: Name, Gender, DOB, Category, etc.
  • 2. Educational Details: Qualification, Year of Passing, University.
  • 3. Communication Details: Address, Mobile No., Email ID.
  • 4. Examination Details: Exam preferences, center, etc.
  • 5. Photo & Signature Upload:
    • Option 1: Combined photo & signature (JPEG, max 150 KB).
    • Option 2: Separate photo (238x306 px, max 50 KB) and signature (350x150 px, max 30 KB).
  • 6. Final Preview & Submit: Review the form and submit. Download the acknowledgment.

Note: Refer to the official portal for format samples and upload guides.

For any technical assistance, use the help desk provided on the official site.
