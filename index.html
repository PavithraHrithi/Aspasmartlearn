How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to apply for government exams in Pondicherry:

  1. Visit the official recruitment portal: recruitment.py.gov.in
  2. Register with valid email and phone number
  3. Login and fill the application form
  4. Upload required documents and pay the application fee
  5. Download the confirmation slip for future reference
